You entered a stasis pod alongside thousands of others, each one of you dreaming of life in a new world. You woke to the violent screams of tearing metal and the howl of rapid decompression.

Thousands of bodies were ripped from their pods and flung into the vast darkness of space. You clawed your way free and hacked the emergency override to seal the breach. You survived, but everyone with Command Authorization to open the pods was dead.

Ark station is your only hope. The orbiting sanctuary is your foothold in a new solar system. But as the Ark Ship draws close, your hope sours.

There is no welcome beacon. No hail from the automatic systems. You dock with the silent hulk and step into a graveyard of cold, dark corridors.

The heart of the station--the reactor--is dead. Backup life support is failing. More than a thousand colonists remain trapped in stasis, unaware their time is running out.

You stand before the silent reactor and pray it will start.
